Sabrina Ellison is currently pursuing an M.Arch 2 at Southern California Institute of Architecture (SCI-Arc) after completing her Bachelor of Science in Architecture at The Ohio State University. She brings a dynamic blend of traditional and digital expertise to the field. Her experience as an assistant at Paros Architecture + Design under Charles Paros enriched her understanding of the delicate balance between functionality and aesthetics. With a passion for coding and a deep interest in digital design, Sabrina envisions reshaping the human experience through innovative architectural perspectives. Her time at SCI-Arc is marked by a commitment to pushing the boundaries of creativity and contributing to projects that seamlessly integrate imagination and sustainability.
